RUBIK'S CUBE SOLVER

Rubik's Cube is a 3-D combination puzzle invented in 1974 by Hungarian sculptor and professor of architecture Ern≈ë Rubik, Originally called the Magic Cube

The solver provides the user with two options

1) SOLVE MANUALLY

6 Keys have been defined for each face of the cube to rotate them in anti-clockwise direction, thus the user can apply his own algorithm to solve the cube by pressing the corresponding keys.

2) AUTO SOLVER i,e show visualizations of the rotations required to solve the cube

IN this mode if "c" key is pressed the series of rotations required to solve the cube will start,thus guiding the user to solve the scrambled cube

SPECIAL FEATURE

When the user chooses AUTO-SOLVER all the specific rotaions to solve the cube will be displayed and once the cube is solved , the cube will explode into particles with a THANK YOU message
